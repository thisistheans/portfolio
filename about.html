Hi, my name is Annie.

I am a biomedical communicator based in Toronto, Canada.

I visualize complex topics in science and medicine in a understandable, impactful and thoughtful way. Currently, I am completing a Masters of Science in Biomedical Communications (BMC) at University of Toronto.

I believe that effective communication is about making ideas accessible and creating meaningful experiences between the communicative medium and the audience. With a strong background in science and art, and a passion for design and problem-solving, I specialize in creating illustrations, 2D animations and interactive media for research, medical, pharmaceutical and scientific industries.

I would be thrilled to discuss potential collaborations and projects with you!
