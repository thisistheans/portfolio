Dow v. Miller: Medical Legal Visualization

Client
Dr. Leila Lax, Biomedical Communications, University of Toronto
Team members
Sarah Crawley

Intended for use as demonstrative evidence in the Canadian courtroom

Audience
Canadian judge and jury
Media
Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator
Date completed
December 2017
Process
Research and initial conceptualization

Something about research, studying the tissue environment using histology and electron micrographs and understanding the major players in the disease, the origin, progression of disease related to its morphology and physiology.

Co-visualization plan

Something about wanting to integrate tissue cubes/cross-sections in a landscape. Focus on concrete steps in the narrative (pathogenesis, chornic bronchitis, emphysema, overall end result) and having a clear flow of information. Eventually settled on a composition inspired by the branching of trees and incorporating an organic yet editorial feel.
