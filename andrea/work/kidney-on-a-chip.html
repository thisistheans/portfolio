Kidney-on-a-Chip

Master's Research Project

Kidneys are an important organ in our body, but they are particularly difficult to study in developing treatments for kidney-related illnesses. A successful, functioning in vitro kidney model would have huge implications for the public, including faster drug development, a diminished need for animal models, and personalized medicine.

Biomedical engineers at the University of Toronto achieved a big milestone in engineering specialized kidney cells called podocytes. This animation was created so that the clients could communicate and promote their research to both a lay and research audience.
